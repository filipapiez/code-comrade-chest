What We Make

Common custom projects

If it can be tempered, polished or laminated, we can probably make it. A few examples.

Tabletops

Glass tabletops

Protect a wood table or build a piece around a polished glass top.

  • ¼″–½″ tempered, polished edges
  • Beveled, pencil or flat polish
  • Cutouts for legs and pedestals
Shelving

Floating shelves & display glass

Glass shelves with polished edges; floating clips or wall channels.

  • ⅜″ tempered standard
  • Concealed standoff or clip
  • Backlit shelf options
Cabinets

Cabinet door inserts

Clear, reeded, fluted or frosted glass cut to your cabinet door openings.

  • Reeded / fluted glass trending
  • Frosted for hidden storage
  • Cut to ⅛″ tolerance
Splashbacks

Splashbacks & wall cladding

Back-painted glass for kitchen splashbacks or feature walls — seamless, easy to clean, custom color.

  • Back-painted any color
  • Tempered for kitchen use
  • One-piece up to standard glass sizes
One-off

One-off projects

Send a drawing, photo or rough sketch — if it can be cut, drilled and tempered, we'll quote it.

  • Templating for unusual shapes
  • CNC drilling and notching
  • Polished or beveled edges
